Tips and Tricks

Browse help topics

To maintain your own account on RR Parking, you must be 18 years old. If we discover that you are not of legal age to have an account. You'll have one week to confirm your information before your account is disabled.


You can login in to multiple accounts at once if you have more than one. You can swap accounts without having to sign out and back in. To do so, you'll need to have different account settings.

Change your password right away if you suspect someone else is accessing your account. Send an email to RR Parking informing them of the issue. Then look for and remove any unknown devices that have signed into your account.



Select the issue for which you are unable to login in to your account. Then you must choose a reason. Select the most relatable option. Then you'll come across some potential solutions.
